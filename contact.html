Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to common inquiries

Are you open to research collaborations?

Yes! I'm always interested in collaborating on bioinformatics and computational biology projects. Whether it's tool development, data analysis, or novel research, feel free to reach out with your ideas.

Can I use your tools for my research?

Absolutely! All my tools are open-source and free to use. Please check the individual repository licenses for specific terms. If you use them in your research, a citation would be appreciated.

How long does it take to respond to messages?

I typically respond to emails and messages within 24-48 hours. For urgent matters, LinkedIn messages might get a faster response.

Do you offer consulting services?

I'm open to discussing consulting opportunities for bioinformatics projects, tool development, or computational biology analyses. Please reach out with details about your project for discussion.

How can I report bugs or request features?

The best way is to open an issue on the respective GitHub repository. Alternatively, you can use the contact form above with "Bug Report" or "Feature Request" as the subject.
